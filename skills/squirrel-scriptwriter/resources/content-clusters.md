## 5 Series Formats

| Series | Purpose | Title Pattern |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| "Explained by a Squirrel" | Foundational, attract new audiences | [Topic] Explained by a Squirrel |
| "What This Looks Like in Real Life" | Fill application gap | What [Topic] Looks Like in Real Life |
| "The Part Most People Miss" | Fill mechanism gap | The Part Most People Miss About [Topic] |
| "Signs You're Looking At…" | Decision-usefulness | [N] Signs You're Looking at a [Thing] |
| "Squirrel Case File" | Applied topical case | Squirrel Case File: [Event/Trend] |
